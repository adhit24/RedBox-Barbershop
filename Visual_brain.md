# Visual Brain — Redbox Barbershop

---

## 1. Visual Core Idea

**“Modern Classic Masculinity”**

Perpaduan antara:
- Barbershop klasik (heritage, ritual, detail)
- Visual modern (clean, sharp, high contrast)

---

## 2. Visual Principles

**1. Dark Dominance**
- Background cenderung gelap
- Menciptakan kesan premium & fokus

**2. Highlight Precision**
- Area terang fokus ke:
  - rambut
  - wajah
  - detail cutting

**3. Human-Centered**
- Selalu ada manusia:
  - barber
  - customer
- Hindari terlalu banyak elemen kosong

**4. Texture & Depth**
- Gunakan:
  - shadow
  - grain halus
  - lighting contrast

---

## 3. Color System

**Primary:**
- Red → identity, energy, boldness

**Secondary:**
- Black → premium, masculine
- White → clarity, balance

**Accent (optional):**
- Gold / warm tone → subtle luxury

---

## 4. Composition Style

- Close-up haircut detail
- Medium shot (barber at work)
- Before/after transformation
- Rule of thirds (tidak selalu center)

---

## 5. Content Visual Categories

**1. Craft**
- Proses cutting
- Detail fade
- Tools

**2. Result**
- Final haircut
- Before/after

**3. Experience**
- Interior
- Interaction
- Ritual (hot towel, shaving)

**4. Identity**
- Customer confidence
- Lifestyle

---

## 6. Do & Don’t

**DO:**
- Gunakan lighting dramatis
- Fokus ke detail
- Maintain tone gelap

**DON’T:**
- Lighting flat
- Background berantakan
- Warna terlalu ramai
- Template random tanpa sistem

---

## 7. Visual Goal

Membuat audience merasa:
- “Ini tempat serius”
- “Hasilnya rapi & presisi”
- “Gue bakal keliatan lebih proper kalau kesini”
